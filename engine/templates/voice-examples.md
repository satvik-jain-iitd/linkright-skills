# Voice Examples

Template. The Voice Synthesizer fills this. Paired examples that show the voice instead of describing it. Drafting agents copy the feel, scorers calibrate against it.

Each example has three parts. The situation, the owner's raw voice, and the target, the same thing in the configured output language and writing rules. Then one line on why it matches.
