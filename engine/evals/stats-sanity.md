# Eval, Stats Sanity

The Data Analyst runs this, it also validates the W7 output. Every item must pass.

- [ ] Findings weight saves, dwell, and inbound over likes, tied to the owner's goal
- [ ] Small samples are flagged, no trend called on two or three posts
- [ ] Every recommendation cites the data behind it
- [ ] Signal is separated from noise, no overreaction to a short streak
- [ ] The report names what to change and what to leave alone

Pass only if every box is checked.
