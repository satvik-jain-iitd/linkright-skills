# Eval, Interview Coverage

The Interviewer runs this on the transcript before handing off. Every item must pass. A miss means go back and ask more, do not hand off a thin interview.

## Coverage gates

- [ ] The interview ran on a topic the owner was visibly comfortable with
- [ ] Between eight and twelve questions, adaptive, each grown from the last answer
- [ ] At least one critical incident, a specific real event told in detail
- [ ] Laddered to a core value at least once, reached a why the owner could not reduce further
- [ ] One contrarian probe run, the owner's pushback and argument style captured
- [ ] A self narrative captured, the owner framing their own arc in their own words

## Capture quality gates

- [ ] Answers recorded word for word, filler and slang and code switching kept
- [ ] Raw owner words kept separate from interviewer observations
- [ ] No leading, the owner was not handed the answers
- [ ] Both layers present, the transcript shows how they think and preserves how they phrase

## Result

Pass only if every box is checked. On a miss, name the gap and resume the interview.
