# Eval, Voice Authenticity

The Storyteller runs this on the body draft. Scored, not just pass or fail, using the voice profile scoring hooks and the rubric in scoring-rubrics.md. The draft must clear the threshold before handoff.

## Scored against the voice profile

- [ ] Opens on a hard fact or an uncomfortable truth, not a wind up
- [ ] At least one contrast pair, X not Y
- [ ] A section or the post closes on a short principle line, not a sell
- [ ] Rhythm is bursty, short lines next to long ones, not uniform
- [ ] Faith or a real personal stake is present where the topic allows
- [ ] The owner's raw banned words are mapped to their allowed swaps
- [ ] Reads like the owner, matches the do list, avoids the do not list

## Threshold

Voice authenticity and human texture both at or above 8 on the rubric. A draft that misses three or more of the signals above does not read like the owner, send it back to rewrite. Up to five loops, then flag to the Master Evaluator.
