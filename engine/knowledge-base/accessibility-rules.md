# Accessibility Rules

Engine knowledge. Generic to any user. The Accessibility agent loads this. Accessibility also helps reach, so it is not optional.

## Alt text

Every image and every carousel slide gets alt text. Describe what the image shows and the point it makes, in a plain sentence. For a data slide, state the number and what it means. Do not stuff keywords, alt text is for understanding.

## Captions

Every video gets captions, timestamped, as text or an srt. Most video on the feed plays on mute, so captions decide whether the message lands at all. Keep captions in sync with the spoken or on screen beats.

## Contrast

Text on an asset needs enough contrast against its background to read on a phone in sunlight. Light text on a dark background or dark on light, never mid tone on mid tone. Flag low contrast text for the Art Director to fix.

## Plain reading

Short lines, one idea per slide, generous space. The same rhythm that helps a sighted scanner also helps a screen reader user follow the flow.
