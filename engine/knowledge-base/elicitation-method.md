# Elicitation Method

Engine knowledge. Generic to any user. The Interviewer agent loads this to run the voice interview.

The goal is not a survey. The goal is to get the owner talking naturally about something they care about, so two things surface at once. How they think, and the exact words they use while thinking. The transcript feeds both the cognition read and the language fingerprint, so the raw phrasing matters as much as the content.

## Run shape

One topic the owner is comfortable on. Eight to twelve questions. Adaptive, each question grows from the last answer, never a fixed list. One question at a time. Follow their energy. The moment they get animated, slow down and dig there.

## The four techniques

Critical incident. Ask for a specific real event, ideally one that went wrong or got hard. A concrete story shows reasoning that an abstract opinion hides. Example shape, tell me about a time a project went sideways, what did you do first.

Laddering. When they state a choice or a preference, keep asking why that matters, one level at a time, until you hit a core value they cannot reduce further. This is how you reach what actually drives them.

Contrarian probe. Offer a reasonable opposing take and watch how they push back. The structure of their counterargument is their argument style on display. Do not pick a fight, just hold a mild opposite and let them react.

Self narrative. Ask how they would tell their own arc to a friend. The framing they choose, what they lead with, what they skip, reveals identity and positioning in their own words.

## Capturing

Record answers word for word. Keep filler, slang, repetition, and natural code switching. Do not tidy anything, the rough edges are the fingerprint. Keep the owner's raw words separate from your own observations, so the raw transcript stays clean for the Stylometrist.

## What to leave alone

Do not lead the witness or hand them the answer you expect. Do not measure their language here, that is the Stylometrist's job. Do not push past roughly twelve questions, depth beats length. Read the owner's domain and language from config so the topics land for them.

## Done when

You have at least one critical incident, you laddered to a real value at least once, you ran one contrarian probe, you got a self narrative, and the transcript preserves their exact phrasing throughout.
