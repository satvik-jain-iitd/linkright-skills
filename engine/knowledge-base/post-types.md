# Post Types

Engine knowledge. Generic to any user. The Topic Selector and the Distribution Strategist load this. Each type has a psychological trigger, an ideal format, and a structure. The owner's own pillar mix lives in config, this file is the menu.

## The types

Proof of work or thinking. A process, a framework, a behind the scenes. Trigger, authority and curiosity. Best as a document carousel or image plus text.

Data backed insight. A statistic or finding with the owner's own read on it. Trigger, credibility. Best as a document carousel with charts.

Personal story or journey. Vulnerability plus a lesson. Trigger, relatability and emotion. Best as text or a single image.

Contrarian or hot take. Challenge a popular belief. Trigger, debate. Best as text with one bold line, or a stark image.

Listicle or checklist. A numbered, useful set. Trigger, saves and utility. Best as a document carousel.

Lead magnet or resource. A free template or toolkit. Trigger, reciprocity. Best as a document post. Note the link reach penalty, point to a follow or a comment, not an outbound link.

Poll. A quick opinion. Trigger, ego and curiosity about results. Native poll.

Case study or result. Before and after, a real outcome. Trigger, trust and evidence. Best as a document carousel.

Question or conversation starter. Open ended, engagement first. Trigger, the urge to opine. Text or poll. Strong for early comments.

Curated roundup. The best resources or ideas on a theme. Trigger, utility and saves. Document carousel.

Video scripted content. Talking head or animated explainer. Trigger, parasocial connection and the format boost. Native video with captions.

Teardown. A researched breakdown of a real product. Trigger, expertise on display. Document carousel or text with visuals. Note, route real company teardowns through the Ethics and Risk review.

Career navigation. Advice for people navigating their own path. Trigger, relatability and saves. Text or carousel.

## How to use this

Match the chosen topic to the type whose trigger fits the goal, then let the Distribution Strategist pick the format for reach. Read the owner's preferred pillar mix and cadence from config, this file just defines what each type is and how it behaves.
