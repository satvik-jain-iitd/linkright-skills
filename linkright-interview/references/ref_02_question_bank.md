# ref_02 — Question Bank

## Universal PM Questions (applies to all archetypes)

1. Tell me about yourself / walk me through your background.
2. Why this company / why this role?
3. Tell me about a product you built end-to-end. What was the outcome?
4. Describe a time you had to make a decision with incomplete data. What did you do?
5. Tell me about a time a project failed. What did you learn?
6. How do you prioritize when everything is urgent?
7. Describe a conflict with engineering / design / leadership. How did you resolve it?
8. How do you define success for a product you own?
9. What's a product you use daily that you think could be significantly improved?
10. Tell me about a time you influenced without authority.
11. Describe your approach to discovery and user research.
12. How do you handle scope creep or changing requirements mid-sprint?
13. Tell me about a metric you moved. What specifically did you do?
14. Describe your relationship with your engineering team. How do you work with them?
15. Where do you want to be in 3 years? Why does this role fit that path?

## Per-Archetype Question Banks

### Archetype: Growth PM

1. Walk me through a growth loop you designed. What made it sustainable?
2. What's your framework for deciding between acquisition, activation, and retention bets?
3. How do you run an A/B test? What's the minimum bar for shipping?
4. You run an experiment. Results are inconclusive. What do you do?
5. What's your approach to funnel instrumentation from scratch?
6. Describe a time you improved a metric that was moving the wrong direction.
7. How do you balance short-term growth with long-term product health?
8. What acquisition channels have you worked on? What was the CAC tradeoff?
9. How do you think about cohort analysis vs aggregate metrics?
10. You have 3 months to double a specific KPI. Walk me through your plan.

### Archetype: Platform / Infrastructure PM

1. How do you balance internal developer experience with end-user impact?
2. How do you build a roadmap when your customers are internal teams?
3. Describe a time you drove adoption of an internal platform. What was the blocker?
4. How do you handle versioning and backwards compatibility?
5. Walk me through how you'd define SLA targets for a new API.
6. How do you make build vs buy vs partner decisions for infrastructure?
7. How do you measure the ROI of platform investment?
8. Your platform team is seen as a bottleneck. How do you change that perception?
9. Describe how you've worked with security and compliance on a platform product.
10. How do you think about observability and on-call responsibility for your products?

### Archetype: 0→1 / Founding PM

1. Tell me about a product you built from zero. What signal told you it was time to build?
2. How do you validate a hypothesis before writing a single line of code?
3. Describe how you've set up processes from scratch on a new team.
4. How do you decide when to stop iterating on an MVP and invest in scale?
5. Tell me about a time you had to kill a product or feature you built. How did you make that call?
6. How do you do customer discovery when you have no customers yet?
7. What does your first 30/60/90 day plan look like joining a 0→1 role?
8. How do you handle ambiguity in strategy when leadership hasn't defined it yet?
9. Describe a time you had to make a build decision with no data. What happened?
10. What's your approach to defining a go-to-market strategy for a new product?

### Archetype: Enterprise / B2B PM

1. How do you manage a product that serves both SMB and enterprise segments simultaneously?
2. Describe your experience working with a large account's product feedback. How did you balance their requests vs roadmap?
3. How do you work with sales to gather product requirements without overfitting to one customer?
4. Walk me through how you'd approach a major enterprise customer asking for a custom feature.
5. Describe a time you had to navigate procurement or compliance requirements in product decisions.
6. How do you think about packaging and pricing for a B2B SaaS product?
7. How do you define and track NPS / CSAT / retention for an enterprise product?
8. Describe your approach to customer success handoff after a feature ships.
9. How do you build for a buying committee vs an end user who are different people?
10. Tell me about a renewal or expansion motion you influenced with product decisions.

### Archetype: Consumer / B2C PM

1. Tell me about a consumer product moment you're proud of. What made it delightful?
2. How do you think about notification design and the risk of notification fatigue?
3. Describe your approach to onboarding design. What metrics do you optimize?
4. How do you think about viral coefficients and social sharing mechanics?
5. Describe a personalization system you've designed or improved.
6. How do you balance business goals (monetization, ads) with user experience?
7. Walk me through a push notification strategy for a re-engagement campaign.
8. How do you handle A/B test results that are good for the business but hurt some users?
9. Tell me about a consumer behavior change you designed for. What was hard about it?
10. Describe your process for writing product copy and microcopy. Who's involved?

### Archetype: Data / AI PM

1. Walk me through a product decision where data analysis led you somewhere surprising.
2. How do you decide when to trust a model's output and when to override it?
3. What's your process for working with data science on a prediction feature?
4. Describe your approach to model evaluation and how you define success metrics.
5. How do you communicate uncertainty and confidence intervals to stakeholders?
6. Tell me about a time you had to kill or retrain a model. What happened?
7. How do you handle bias and fairness considerations in AI features?
8. Walk me through how you'd define the MVP for an AI-powered feature.
9. How do you explain model behavior to customers who ask "why did it do that?"
10. Describe your approach to data labeling and training data quality.

### Archetype: Design-Led PM

1. How do you collaborate with design to avoid "design by committee"?
2. Tell me about a time you pushed back on a design decision. What happened?
3. How do you balance user research insights with business constraints in design?
4. Walk me through your design review process. How do you give useful feedback?
5. Describe a time you simplified a complex user flow. What was the outcome?
6. How do you handle disagreements between design and engineering on implementation fidelity?
7. Tell me about a time accessibility requirements changed your product direction.
8. How do you decide when to do qualitative vs quantitative research?
9. What's your process for running a usability test? Who attends?
10. Describe how you handle feature requests from users that conflict with design vision.

### Archetype: Marketplace / Two-Sided PM

1. How do you manage the chicken-and-egg problem in a two-sided marketplace?
2. Describe how you've balanced supply and demand side priorities in roadmap planning.
3. How do you define and track liquidity for a marketplace?
4. Walk me through how you'd approach a new geographic expansion.
5. How do you think about trust and safety in a marketplace product?
6. Describe a fraud or abuse pattern you've handled at the product level.
7. How do you design incentive structures that don't create perverse behavior?
8. How do you measure marketplace health beyond GMV?
9. Tell me about a time you had to make a decision that hurt one side of the marketplace to help the other.
10. How do you think about search and ranking in a two-sided context?

## Behavioral Question Patterns

All archetypes — STAR format (Situation, Task, Action, Result):

**Conflict**: "Describe a conflict with [stakeholder]. How did you resolve it?"
**Failure**: "Tell me about a time a project or product failed."
**Influence**: "Tell me about a time you influenced without authority."
**Ambiguity**: "Describe a time you had to decide with incomplete information."
**Leadership**: "Tell me about a time you led through a difficult situation."
